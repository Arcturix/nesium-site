Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Nesium.

What kinds of workflows can you automate?

We can automate anything - any pain points, repetitive tasks, or manual processes that slow your team down. If it's something you do regularly, we can build an automation for it.

Examples include: quoting and proposal generation, booking pipelines and scheduling, automated reminders and follow-ups, reporting and data aggregation, lead routing and qualification, content production and distribution, invoice processing and payment workflows, customer onboarding sequences, inventory management, email and communication workflows, data synchronization between systems, and much more—all tailored to your specific stack and business goals.

How long does a typical implementation take?
Most projects ship in 1–3 weeks depending on scope and integrations. We start with a one‑page plan and ship iteratively.
Which tools and platforms do you support?
We commonly use n8n, Zapier, Make, OpenAI, Stripe, Google Workspace, Slack and popular CRMs. We also build custom connectors when needed.
How do you handle data privacy and security?
We follow least‑privilege access, encrypt credentials, and keep sensitive data within your systems whenever possible. We can work with your IT policies.
What does pricing look like?
Automations start from £500 a month. We offer fixed‑price scopes for well‑defined workflows, or monthly retainers for ongoing automation. We'll recommend the most cost‑effective option in your plan.
What do you need from us to get started?
A short discovery call, access to relevant tools, and example inputs/outputs. We’ll handle the rest and share a clear rollout plan.
